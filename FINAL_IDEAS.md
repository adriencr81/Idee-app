## 2️⃣ CYCLE SPORT — 8.0+/10 ⭐ **PRIORITÉ #2**

### Elevator Pitch
**"Cycle Sync : Optimize your athletic performance by understanding your cycle phases."**

### Why This Works
- ✅ **Angle SPORT (not fertility)** — athletic performance optimization
- ✅ **High willingness to pay** — athletes spend $100+/mo on optimization
- ✅ **Privacy moat** — RGPD-first (Clue fails here: exported data to marketplace)
- ✅ **Zero competitor** — No app combines cycle tracking + athletic performance
- ✅ **Niche but passionate** — Women athletes = high-engagement segment

### Real Pain Point (Found)
- Women athletes: "My cycle affects my strength 40%, endurance 60% — where's the app to optimize this ?"
- Clue = period/contraception tracker (not athlete-focused)
- WHOOP = recovery tracking (ignores cycle)
- Strava = training log (doesn't factor cycle)
- **GAP = Cycle + Athletic Performance = missing**

### Features MVP
1. **Cycle phase detection** — auto-detect follicular, ovulation, luteal, menstrual
2. **Performance optimization guide** — "This week: peak strength, do heavy lifting"
3. **Nutrition timing** — carbs/protein recommendations by phase
4. **Training periodization** — suggest workout type by cycle phase
5. **Wearable integration** — Apple Watch, Garmin (HRV, recovery)
6. **Privacy-first** — RGPD compliant, no data marketplace, local storage option

### Pricing Model
- **Free** : cycle tracking + basic education
- **Premium** : €9,99/mo (AI coach, periodization, nutrition timing)

### Target Market
- Women cyclists, runners, CrossFit athletes, fitness enthusiasts
- TAM: 850k women cyclists France, 5.5M women in sports Europe
- High-paying segment (athletes = premium users)

### Why It Won't Get Copied
- Privacy = moat (once trust gained, very sticky)
- Sports science expertise = defensible
- Athlete community = distribution advantage

### Competitive Advantage
| Competitor | Focus | Problem | Your Win |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clue | Period tracking | Not athlete-focused, privacy issues | Athletic optimization |
| WHOOP | Recovery | Ignores cycle | Cycle-native design |
| Strava | Training log | No cycle tracking | Cycle integration |
| Apple Fitness | General | Not cycle-aware | Cycle-specific coaching |

## 📚 KEY LEARNINGS FROM 40-IDEA VALIDATION

### Why These 2 Were Chosen (From 40 ideas)

**Rejected Ideas & Why :**
- ❌ **Golf Tracker** (7.0) : Garmin + Apple Watch = hardware platform lock-in
- ❌ **TikTok Résumés** (7.5) : TikTok tried & killed it (two-sided market failure)
- ❌ **Live Seller Assistant** (8.0) : TikTok created native version (platform risk)
- ❌ **Journal Anxiété TCC** (7.5) : Rooted ($1M+) + Wysa ($10M+) dominate

### Validation Red Flags (That Killed Ideas)

1. **Platform owns the feature** → TikTok Seller Assistant native → REJECT
2. **Two-sided market broken** → TikTok Résumés (candidates vs recruiters misaligned) → REJECT
3. **Hardware incumbent lock-in** → Garmin golf + Apple Watch → REJECT or pivot
4. **Leader failed publicly** → TikTok tried Résumés → investigate why
5. **Viralité ≠ Produit** → "46% Gen Z found jobs via TikTok" was ORGANIC, not app-mediated

### Validation Rules That Worked

**Rule 1: "Check if the leader tried & failed"**
- TikTok Résumés → TikTok killed it
- Signal: Market structure is fundamentally broken

**Rule 2: "Verify two-sided alignment"**
- TikTok Résumés → Candidates wanted it, recruiters didn't (misaligned)
- Golf Tracker → Apple/Garmin users locked into platforms

**Rule 3: "Concurrence poorly executed = opportunity"**
- Golf (18Birdies) → Market leader BUT pop-up hell + broken handicap
- Cycle (Clue) → Market leader BUT privacy lies (exported to data marketplace)

**Rule 4: "Tendance ≠ Demande produit"**
- Kaizen viral on TikTok BUT no app exists (products lag trends)
- Cycle sport = athletes want it, zero app combines cycle + performance

**Rule 5: "Single-sided markets > Two-sided markets"**
- Kaizen : users want it (single-sided) ✅
- Cycle Sport : athletes want it (single-sided) ✅
- TikTok Résumés : candidates want it, recruiters don't (two-sided fail) ❌
