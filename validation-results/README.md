### Scoring Methodology

Each idea scored on 6 dimensions:
- **Tendance montante** - Explicit TikTok, Google Trends, Reddit signals
- **Concurrence** - Gap vs existing solutions
- **TAM** - Market size (France focus)
- **Gap français** - Local differentiation opportunity
- **Defensibilité** - Moat, science backing, regulation
- **Urgence** - Window of opportunity open/closed

**Final score = Average of 6 dimensions**

## 🔴 Red Flags Observed

❌ **Avoid:**
- Apps too complex (>5 core features)
- Saturated markets without clear differentiation
- TAM <50k potential users in France
- Zero customer demand validation

✅ **Prioritize:**
- Explicit TikTok trends
- Specific unresolved pain points
- Clear distribution channels (influencers ready)
- Validated willingness to pay
